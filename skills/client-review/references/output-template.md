## Style rules
- Keep the entire memo short enough to scan in 1-2 minutes.
- Lead with the household's real priorities.
- Keep jargon low unless the client is highly sophisticated.
- Explain disappointing performance directly and calmly.
- Make actions specific, proportional, and time-bound.
- Separate factual recap from recommendations.
- Do not add extra sections unless the user asks.
