# Sector Metrics Reference

Use this file when an earnings preview needs help identifying the few operating metrics most likely to move the stock.

## Software / SaaS
- ARR / annual recurring revenue
- Net revenue retention
- Billings
- Remaining performance obligations (RPO)
- Customer adds / enterprise customer count
- Gross margin and operating margin
- Sales efficiency / CAC payback when relevant

## Internet / marketplace
- GMV
- Active buyers / sellers / users
- Take rate
- Ad load / ad monetization
- Contribution margin
- Geography mix

## Retail / consumer
- Same-store sales
- Traffic
- Basket size / average ticket
- Gross margin
- Inventory days / markdown intensity
- Category and channel mix

## Industrials
- Orders
- Backlog
- Book-to-bill
- Price vs. volume
- Segment margin
- Free cash flow conversion

## Semiconductors / hardware
- Revenue by end market
- Gross margin
- Inventory correction / channel inventory
- Utilization
- ASP trend
- Design win or production ramp commentary

## Financials
- Net interest margin
- Deposit growth and deposit costs
- Loan growth
- Credit quality / NPLs / charge-offs
- Reserve build / release
- Capital return

## Healthcare / medtech / biotech
- Script trends
- Procedure or patient volume
- Reimbursement commentary
- Product mix
- Pipeline timing / trial milestones
- Guidance on launches or label expansion

## Energy / materials
- Production volume
- Realized price
- Unit costs
- Utilization
- Hedging impact
- Capital spending and free cash flow

Rule of thumb: choose the 3-5 metrics that best explain why investors own or avoid the stock. Ignore the rest.
